Welcome Language Learner!

At Access Language Academy we believe that a personalized approach is best. We work with our students and create a personalized learning experience. Our teachers are certified and have a track record of success. It is never to late to learn a language and with our proven methods we will make the process memorable and enjoyable. Our goal oriented approach lets the student know they are making progress. Join us in this journey to the fluent you.

Features Of Our Program

Structured Conversation

Our instructors lead conversations with students. These conversations are designed to enforce the concepts discussed in class. Students will be coached on common phrases, vocabulary and expressions they can use to improve their conversation.

More Info

Grammar Immersion

Grammar is taught through examples in there context. Students must use these grammar forms to express themselves effectively. Exercises are created to immerse the students in the grammar taught. The end goal is to use the grammar forms without thinking, automatically choosing the correct forms.

More Info

Placement Exams

Our placement exam is designed to test the level of our students. The placement exam is internationally recognized and can be used as proof of a students' language level. Before classes begin students will be tested on their level. The tests will be used to create a personalized learning plan for the student.

More Info

Reading Comprehension

Students will increase their reading comprehension through a select literature plan. The readings will be acompanied by discussion of the material and the vocabulary used. Students will increase their reading comprehension in a group setting.
